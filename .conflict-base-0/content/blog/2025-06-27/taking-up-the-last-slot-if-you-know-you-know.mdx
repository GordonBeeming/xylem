Some things just belong together. Like that last piece of a puzzle clicking into place, or squeezing one final item into an already-full bag and somehow making it work.

There's a specific satisfaction in slotting into exactly the right space at exactly the right time. Not forcing it. Just... timing it well.

Call it opportunistic if you want. I'd call it efficient.

If you're nodding right now, you already get it. You've been there - spotted the opening, waited for the moment, and taken it. No fuss, no drama. Just the quiet satisfaction of knowing you read the situation correctly.

Especially when it's the last spot going.

So here's to never leaving a space empty when it could be filled. Particularly that final one. The one everyone else missed.

You know who you are.
