So we use [CheckPoint VPN](http://www.checkpoint.com/resources/virtual-private-network/), but the most frustrating part is that when a new version on windows is released the CheckPoint VPN client is incompatible with it and makes your computer weird when you do try use it.
 
For a while now I (and others) have been waiting for a client for windows 8.1. Very recently I found out that this functionality is baked into Windows 8.1 already and we don't have to wait ![Smile](https://gordonbeeming.com/images/emoticons/smile.svg). Here's how to create the VPN connection
 
Open the Charms bar (slide from the right or Win + C) and click Settings
 
[![step 1](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/dfcd2e60da5bad407ab3f957c8f7a77306d4a448/1b1fc165-4a72-41d5-9f48-c8dfb3f16bb7.png "step 1")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f103cffd2c20780f98547265b4c423285f24f329/37ed910a-6e23-46d1-ad31-321815309175.png)
 
Select Change PC Settings
 
[![step 2](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/ade8bddb618749ebfdf63f906eb97eb16ef551dd/d83c8689-8c82-4b38-9800-772286351314.png "step 2")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/a28bdfe7a1dd29e93eae3938b1e0970c17fa7014/3b067aa6-bfb3-4788-9a16-1013bb1aea03.png)
 
Click on Network
 
[![step 3](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/c23388f657da9e076036b3f6e2884280f913c859/50c39f5f-ece5-48e1-ac4d-62a86464c7b0.png "step 3")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/ccc362686aecae82c967bb9434b16dda8ba8d793/70507f0d-6d3a-4319-8793-bc76cf2f62af.png)
 
Under connections select Add a VPN connection
 
[![step 4](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/c72ff847990b6a30e68302b8d9d2c43d88551f09/508d433f-3c2b-445c-b3c1-16a032c3c798.png "step 4")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/1b2b4a279d4d89a36523170724e4341dafb0dc38/6d25c4b9-2ca6-4f6a-bb8b-05cf77f3f5ec.png)
 
Choose Check Point VPN as the provider, enter a name for the connection and also enter the server name or address and click Save
 
[![step 5](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/b8c58ed4081b5a8f6afc615ce1c3973d9a7dcdbb/86473637-c22d-4711-a804-b27553a1a769.png "step 5")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/cb4dd3d8da20f9c6cc5c37a2ebead063d568f675/0d0d947f-62aa-4ac2-9c31-edb4a617cdfe.png)
 
You should see your connection in the Connections page now
 
[![step 6](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/293a27734d90b6fb146bd9b7255ed2998fdd2da8/58a182ca-f161-4fa0-b466-020cd3f2116a.png "step 6")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/ce42ab3144ea5a5df4b0408b65714c471bfce012/a4019093-71e0-40cd-950a-a29c00d161da.png)
 
Head back to the charms bar and click Networks
 
[![step 7](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/ad9539d68737b662acd2189258b058410d7f5126/0291b782-a99e-4b30-91ec-b5c7376062d6.png "step 7")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/1e63f397e381ede64e2f0e71cff3226536933a39/69405d4b-dd4d-4eb7-8376-92d30cea8fac.png)
 
Under Connections you will see your VPN connection, Click on it
 
[![step 8](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/02c2a977008dc3882d76d53cc0cc91cb55645220/ffd94d6c-35b3-455c-b828-8d124aef1708.png "step 8")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f4b2c6b029f2cd38729bd1df1fb7a467564fa5e7/cac9e583-8eb2-4499-bd24-2c3371145940.png)
 
Click on Connect
 
[![step 9](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/641077d0ddd959ffc7baf267ed182d29daf992ac/27849013-8b32-4447-b55b-e7d178a45e3b.png "step 9")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/c951b5d931d5678ea616e25473b159426638a80e/ed4d08cd-ea0f-43f4-993b-676739756bf6.png)
 
Select the authentication method (I'll use Username and Password), Click Next
 
[![step 10](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/ef193592c5fad35c877972154923bcdf1af9ba09/35238d32-9508-4a76-ad34-2cdf64684503.png "step 10")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/b7fda9ca38e62e81cef1c4a2dae368db6a3ebf62/3b8df14b-c380-49f3-af30-e54e32420f2a.png)
 
Enter the Username and Password and click OK
 
[![step 11](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/de74671ab6ff0fabf4d7d8d2ee6e33dffbbe7ed5/e0c52c42-8a4a-4d3f-98b7-f5756f8764e3.png "step 11")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/0c355c01b9fce22d6ce450e84edbbaf136c0dae5/80842dbf-79fe-4169-8f0c-c220b4b56abe.png)
 
You will now be connected
 
[![step 12](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/69834c15343b8548295c8b5c8bf9ff6e6a01fcdd/77811297-d7c3-4d90-8641-8e82f9cc7337.png "step 12")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f140ed6f5bb8d7a2afa63e0912180016c15e1337/6a5699ee-d547-4c40-af71-47afba29f457.png)
 
It's that simple, no more waiting around for VPN providers to update their clients ![Smile](https://gordonbeeming.com/images/emoticons/smile.svg)
