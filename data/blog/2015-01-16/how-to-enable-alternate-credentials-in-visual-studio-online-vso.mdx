## Your VSO Account
 
Ok so obviously the first you need is to have a VSO account. If you have one already, awesome, continue on and skip this section. 
 
If not then you can get at [http://www.visualstudio.com/](http://www.visualstudio.com/ "http://www.visualstudio.com/"). All you need to do is click on *Sign up*, fill in all of one field for what you want your account name to be and then click *Create Account*.
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f3f39d8e3470f82965a420e743bd53b5212515ef/00712b9e-eb37-47c2-a8b3-a59f84fd0430.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f3f39d8e3470f82965a420e743bd53b5212515ef/3c168807-d603-4d78-93ce-c8909301d2dd.png)
     
In less than a minute (exactly 11 seconds when making this post) you will see the page to create your first VSO Project for this account.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-16-42](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/a29e0b9bd89fd09ee3d6d96a7a4c3d78516df3f1/5dad18f4-1c3b-4afb-9685-18e4e7532a2f.png "2015-01-16_21-16-42")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f3460e1ec82a34aae1450c80d614f151419c7809/fcab158f-3f3b-4cb1-acde-70f781f55783.png)
 
## Configuring Alternate Credentials
 
In the top right click on you name and then on the *My profile* link.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-21-29](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/56ad3eb61f087b64043bcc68fe2454565250ec3a/26a7172a-2145-4464-965f-82bea7b7d1d5.png "2015-01-16_21-21-29")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/68f22b292fdbda6538b4db4a9c7e4ddc7c46bb5b/0abc925f-3242-4e40-8b39-df00abffd0b1.png)
 
When the dialog pops open you will need to click on the *Credentials* tab and then on *Enable alternate credentials*.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-22-58](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/dbd43f03375fe7b7512b2630f0993a995f426fdc/47d7c038-4e42-45b7-922c-b237faab9f9e.png "2015-01-16_21-22-58")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/75a8e9fe2051f263220e183234de4807b33f6e71/541c7bcc-a503-432d-adda-3705de508ccf.png)
 
At this point you can enter a secondary user name if you want and you will also use you will be required to enter a password and confirm that password and then click *Save changes*.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-25-35](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/199d0d1b6dd345a4c7e600df591ea891991a45a5/782c0437-17cb-40cd-9ef1-672ec6638f63.png "2015-01-16_21-25-35")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/9f9039607a13c43e81b57868c88f0b05954b4449/54de35c6-40c7-478b-9cda-8164441625d9.png)
 
This will allow you to connect to VSO using [basic authentication](http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms789031%28v=vs.110%29.aspx). 
 
## Disabling Alternate Credentials
 
You can at any point come back to this dialog and click on *Disable alternate credentials* if you want to have alternate credentials enabled anymore.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-33-37](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/e17a3ccc1fe874fa158d009ddbc9a3aebf05c6d9/73e3889d-cd0a-4d44-8857-5ed3f0251f8c.png "2015-01-16_21-33-37")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/cd9a6b49c35fff2938118ed11a7c722abf5b0152/fe46b0e7-7caa-422f-b3e8-9b8975651f70.png)
      
and then again click *Save changes*.
 
[![2015-01-16_21-34-30](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/2139cbd7c46b2af9ad38074e8796fa80736f682e/18216ff9-f185-42ad-8ce6-24662e2be11c.png "2015-01-16_21-34-30")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/3ea64c5d2c8f6f6a4e7d02897d45fc17fffc7b23/be863430-3cf7-4d66-a949-8a56f1ef2f36.png)
 
Alternate credentials will now be disabled.
