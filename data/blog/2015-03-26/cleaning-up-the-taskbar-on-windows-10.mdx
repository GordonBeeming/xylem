## Hide Task View button
 
To hide the Task View button simple right click on the Taskbar and then un tick **Show Task View button**.
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/70912c98b7fbf720bd93ce880fba0fb62b8d4a9d/c41185d0-37ea-4d81-b561-d7ef28e752c9.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/57762472dd8d63d7b44e6c55cd37509d68803bf2/3cc084c2-d6ee-4224-83c8-d1122dd7b905.png)
