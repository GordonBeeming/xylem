To get this series started we will start with the most basic of activities which is creating a new Visual Studio Online account. Navigate to [http://www.visualstudio.com/](http://www.visualstudio.com/ "http://www.visualstudio.com/") in your favorite browser 
 
<Figure key="/images/834681ab7dda40e8b2f9a331425f5d26.png" src="/images/834681ab7dda40e8b2f9a331425f5d26.png" alt="Visual Studio homepage" width="0" height="0" caption="Visual Studio Homepage" /> 
 
Click **Sign Up** on the tile on the right
 
<Figure key="/images/9746f60f95d84c53af90e5cc332550b4.png" src="/images/9746f60f95d84c53af90e5cc332550b4.png" alt="Visual Studio homepage with 'Sign Up' button highlighted" width="0" height="0" caption="Visual Studio Homepage - Sign Up" /> 
 
You will then be asked to sign in if you not already and then be redirected to the [Create a Visual Studio Online Account](https://app.vssps.visualstudio.com/profile/account?account=true) page. Enter your account name, note you can click on **Change options** to select a different region to host your Team Project Collection in which currently supports *South Central US* and *West Europe*. Click **Create Account** when you done and ready to continue
 
<Figure key="/images/fa25744420da459d97a061a724cb8d9d.png" src="/images/fa25744420da459d97a061a724cb8d9d.png" alt="Create a Visual Studio Online Account page" width="0" height="0" caption="Create VSO Account Page" /> 
 
It will then think for a couple minutes
 
<Figure key="/images/ab26d52722ca408ba13144435ec79ae5.png" src="/images/ab26d52722ca408ba13144435ec79ae5.png" alt="Visual Studio Online account creation progress" width="0" height="0" caption="VSO Account Creation Progress" /> 
 
and then redirect to your account page
 
<Figure key="/images/e5ff0d474f5542e9975cf225e52b51ad.png" src="/images/e5ff0d474f5542e9975cf225e52b51ad.png" alt="Visual Studio Online account page" width="0" height="0" caption="VSO Account Page" /> 
 
You are now done with creating a Visual Studio Online account
