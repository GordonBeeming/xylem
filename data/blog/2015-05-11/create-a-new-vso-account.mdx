This post is part of an overall [Series on Visual Studio Online](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/series/Visual-Studio-Online).
 
To get this series started we will start with the most basic of activities which is creating a new Visual Studio Online account. Navigate to [http://www.visualstudio.com/](http://www.visualstudio.com/ "http://www.visualstudio.com/") in your favorite browser 
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/0c98f89fae847c8e781fff731c1ed8db1cc3bc69/92bd768a-bb0d-433d-a5fa-1b4835525897.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/82e395a604d4ab2d4ae79d4b35d469ab745041dc/a360a90d-1272-468b-8a79-26f389944b1f.png) 
 
Click **Sign Up** on the tile on the right
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/11d47add1286a54836d9e5af98e20e4da209249d/64b8027b-c17f-4312-a14b-205911636fee.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/50f29ae1b85611881c6835f46f6cf1deee3ec303/35e7a4a5-7ec3-4511-a6fd-0afb7c1c9ae0.png) 
 
You will then be asked to sign in if you not already and then be redirected to the [Create a Visual Studio Online Account](https://app.vssps.visualstudio.com/profile/account?account=true) page. Enter your account name, note you can click on **Change options** to select a different region to host your Team Project Collection in which currently supports *South Central US* and *West Europe*. Click **Create Account** when you done and ready to continue
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/2e4de444611d1ea8d78716f09df99dc19cc8ff57/4fd95146-39b0-4834-b778-eafec2b45b94.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/0cc2e985e69fbe052442dbafaa2adf9abdf38da6/b6ed5dcb-cbed-41de-b6a4-1071d891b8b8.png) 
 
It will then think for a couple minutes
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/771ecc16c0324d4e2cd1f428ffa8a102836f1c69/7d4d49ed-69d8-436c-89ea-7fab77d8f951.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/3b58319da8b504fac1adc468446662dc7953d4b5/36781037-9fbd-4f7a-a401-98e4449f3153.png) 
 
and then redirect to your account page
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/14e990b9eba9b642c089995ee066a7f3a998a052/c859a0b9-96ba-41f5-b261-4d0ce81c9cf4.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/db142c98509a422adcbda98c52968db8ab26dd89/7b45e984-5580-4bfc-9119-dd5a867828e1.png) 
 
You are now done with creating a Visual Studio Online account
