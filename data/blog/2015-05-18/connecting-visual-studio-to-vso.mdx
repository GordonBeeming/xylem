## Long Way
 
After you have a new Team Project in VSO you would eventually want to connect your Visual Studio to it. Open Visual Studio
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-png/image/1000/0/fc66fa6e4a1959c0eb5fbb86d6dc88e5e716703e/014d3671-e27b-44b2-9d54-d2d9c1869c4b.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-png/image/1000/0/7a4a4f09addc458c4a9383adf1fdc0ba9e0b0c41/34781cbb-1e45-4400-a4f6-26bf7ecd7c68.png) [![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/8bea0e71ddc1f53dc950da0b6ed4efdabdb9d66f/dacd6b67-e9a8-4134-8c3c-3d2eeb70bb23.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f7cfd569d33ddf3309f7b181040b06257693765c/c517fe74-5645-48e2-9705-42cba32ba158.png) 
 
Open Team Explorer by click **View** and then **Team Explorer**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/5e111fce56cfcbaf01ab754476ac87e78f3f1eb8/a34a7d83-7611-4303-82c6-f901c94b0e9b.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/6297eacaf54d16fa4fc54fe846c3db2c392c3c67/65823903-b9ad-45be-818f-3fe0c31be6c5.png) 
 
When Team Explorer pops up click on the **Connect to Team Projects** button
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/a1bbd0c93b9179662ab59f1166f7f976ae4f45ae/7b1e238a-5867-4b95-b90e-95d36dd90043.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f49b4fffc2d388d7ef6f8df3ccb254b9adcd5e10/6a418422-4ad0-48db-b60f-dc3aadfa5c52.png) 
 
and then click on **Select Team Projects**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/b6618f6226a6de02f432136fd3df0b9981c347bb/a61459d2-2e92-470c-9d18-892a2ec6253b.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/f27e066b9fb3bf003b626ba8ebc0ee7734cfc05b/5b33c84b-28fc-492e-90ab-597985ae2080.png) 
 
Click on **Servers**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/72633fb892f960713852e12879500e11894479ab/cec28f1e-b1c3-4031-9255-b78e3d485369.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/a0c544cdaa6c9452549afa6886b8e30f496adb9f/5b886660-4d98-43f9-a893-647bddf1faee.png) 
        
Visual Studio conveniently loads a list of your servers connected to VSO for you if you signed in, if you don't see your newly created account in the list click **Add**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/6e7b00a56af1eb6ad1ad8e9a0836526c8ea80199/49accab3-94a8-42b8-b7dc-f99027377403.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/d04d9d03a7788680e0c1af180cfa47aaa0393667/bf965d08-a0ad-4cf4-b857-aa613c95f148.png) 
 
Enter the full url to your account as it was on your account page and click **OK**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/1fb7dddae03f90091f5bd264d0b5234c5af4c537/4d2352fe-1a9d-4a4f-a28c-15b45c4ed09d.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/bbaae9afd11b21e45a7270055c1f548991673131/c69067e7-93a2-4f97-a571-8cbd2b1feb35.png) 
 
You account will now exists in the servers list, click Close and then the *Connect to Team Foundation Server* window will auto load your Team Project Collection and it's Team Projects, tick the checkbox next to the Team Projects you want to be able to connect from in Visual Studio and click **Connect**
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/190f091d5dc58454a7bd974b4f3ae260335a9039/448cd0df-c976-4de3-8a6f-18c1dd01cf93.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/8b2ea9f14ce06a433577d41f6af851f91e1bd3cf/a821ac78-4efb-45d6-95b4-a4a3b707357e.png) 
   
Your Team Project is now connected to Visual Studio and you can now setup your builds, clone code repositories, etc
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/bd4bf7caefc3a26342bfc1451a33d1be1a457df9/a1590abc-4901-4fcb-a821-99cea10271ba.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/acb6353019eeba619e0d393f010b5e594908864d/83a27ff0-fe03-4811-a360-6314ecf26051.png) 
 
Slightly more steps than before.
 
## Short Way
 
Of course if you wanted to open the project the easy way you could simple navigate to the Team Project and click on **Open in Visual Studio** 
 
[![image](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/8ba6e59fb7ac2bf24b834313674e54268de59b88/87bf2d87-19ad-46a6-8e99-4fdad9e9c99a.png "image")](https://gordonbeeming.com/blog/binaries/stream/image-jpg/image/1000/0/8010f623dd26f6225be8c5073bba0d09b5c90d38/9aa9c14e-23a9-4414-b229-5992ef48a853.png) 
 
this would then launch a new instance of [Visual Studio](https://www.visualstudio.com/) and connects to your Team Project
