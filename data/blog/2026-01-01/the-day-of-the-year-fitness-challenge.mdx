I was thinking about how to kick off the year with a bit more consistency in my fitness. I'm definitely not at peak fitness right now, so I wanted something that scales. I hit on this idea: **The Day of the Year Challenge.** The logic is simple‚Äîwhatever the day of the year is, that's your number. Today is January 1st, so the number is 1. By December 31st, the number is 365.

## The Original Plan: Pushups ‚ùå

Initially, I was going to do pushups. I figured starting at 1 and ending at 365 would be an epic way to build strength. But then I started doing the "architect math" on it:

* **Overuse Risk:** Doing 300+ pushups every single day by December is a massive load on the shoulders and elbows.
* **Fitness Gap:** Since I'm starting from a low baseline, jumping into high-volume daily pushups is a recipe for an injury that would side-line me by March.
* **Recovery:** Muscles need rest, and pushups are quite taxing on the central nervous system when you do them every single day without a break.

## The Pivot: Skipping and Sit-ups ‚úÖ

I decided to pivot to **skipping**. It's incredible cardio, but doing 1 "skip" felt a bit useless. So, for me, the number represents **seconds**. 

* **Today:** 1 second of skipping.
* **Dec 31st:** 365 seconds (just over 6 minutes).

I mentioned this to my wife, [Tiani](https://tianibeeming.com), and she thought it sounded like a cool idea, but she decided to join in a bit differently. She's not a fan of the rope, so she's going to do **sit-ups**, and for her, the number is the actual **count**. 

* **Today:** 1 sit-up.
* **Dec 31st:** 365 sit-ups.

## How it's going to work

To keep us honest and ensure we don't just "go through the motions," we've added a little bit of a "punishment" or tax for stopping. Think of it as a retry policy for a failing API‚Äîyou can't just let the request drop!

### The Rules

* ‚úÖ **Gordon (Skipping):** If I trip on the rope or stop before my time is up, I have to add a **10-second penalty** to the total for that day. 
* ‚úÖ **Tiani (Sit-ups):** If she pauses or breaks her set, she has to add **5 extra reps** to her total.
* ‚ö†Ô∏è **The Streak:** If we miss a day, we have to "back-fill" the missed day's numbers on top of the current day. No technical debt allowed!
